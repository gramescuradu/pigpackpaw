About the Game

Challenge the AI in this unique twist on the classic Tic-Tac-Toe! Play across multiple game tables simultaneously, where your moves mirror across all boards at once.

  • Play against an adaptive AI that gets tougher when you win and easier when you lose
  • Score points for consecutive cells on rows, columns, or diagonals
  • Multiple game modes with different table sizes and configurations
  • Track your statistics across all game types
  • Special Halloween-themed version available

Scoring System

3 consecutive cells 1 point
4 consecutive cells 5 points
5 consecutive cells 25 points
6 consecutive cells 125 points

Pro Tip: When you move on one table, the move is automatically made on the cell with the same ID across all tables. Plan your strategy carefully!
